About

Three summers, six locations. This site will tell you about all the locations I have visited over the past three summers. In the summer of 2021, I went to Colorado, and visited the Rocky Moutain National Park. The trip included a lot of sightseeing and hiking. In 2022, I went to Duluth, Minnesota and saw Lake Superior. Almost felt like I was seeing an actual ocean. Finally, in the summer of 2023, I went to Wyoming and visited Grand Teton National Park and Yellowstone National Park. That same summer, I also went to South Dakota and visted Badlands National Park and saw Mt. Rushmore.
